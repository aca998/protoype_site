History of Apple

Apple Computer Company was founded April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs,Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne as a business partnership.Wayne sold his share back within 12 days and Apple was incorporated as Apple Computer,Inc on January 3, 1977 without Wayne.Wayne sold his share of the company back to Jobs and Wozniak for 800$.During the first five years of operations, revenues grew exponentially, doubling about every four months.In 2018 Apple Inc is worth about $945 billion and is on the verge of becoming the first American company to be worth $1 trillion.

Apple Computer 1 also known later as the Apple I is a desktop computer released by the Apple Computer Companu in 1976.It was deigned and hand-built by Steve Wozniak.The idea of selling the computer came from Steve Jobs.The apple I was Apple's first product and finance its creation, Jobs sold gis only motorized means of transportation, a VW Microbus, for few houndred dollars and Steve Wozniak sold his HP-65 calculator for $500.It was demonstrated in July 1976 in Palo Alto, California.The apple I went to sale in July 1976 at a price of US$ 666.66 because Wozniak "liked repeating digits" and because of a one third of markup on the $500 wholesale price.The Apple I's built-in computer terminal circutiry was distinctive.All one needed was a keyboard and a television set.Competing machines such as the Altair 8800 generally wewe programed with front-mounted toggle switches and used indicator lights for output, and had to be extended with separate hardware to allow connection to a computer terminal or a teletypewriter machine.
